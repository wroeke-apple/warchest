Data & method

Source: FEC bulk operating-expenditures file, 2023–2024 cycle (oppexp24), filtered to the principal campaign committees of 10 Senate candidates. Memo line items excluded to avoid double counting; negative/void entries dropped.

Scope caveats: Principal committee only — excludes super PACs, party committees, and joint fundraising committees, which is where a large share of media money flows. Vendor names normalized naively; the same firm may appear under multiple spellings or affiliates. Category tags are keyword heuristics.

Win–loss records reflect presence in this 10-race demo set, not a causal claim about vendor quality. The production scorecard adjusts for district partisan lean and role (a media buyer ≠ a general consultant).

No private data. Everything shown derives from public FEC filings and public statements.
